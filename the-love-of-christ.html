THE LOVE OF CHRIST


Oh motives!
Genuinety!
Purity of Intentions!
Jesus exemplified Love in its true and pure form, generosity, equality, service and loyalty. He dined and even served the very outcast. He invited them into his own banquet. There was never a time that he paid more attention to anyone above the other.
There was never a time that he paid more attention to the fortunate above the less privileged. There was never a time that he honored the famous and despised the lonely. He simply lived for everyone! We can become... More like these. Funny enough, this lifestyle of Jesus is fast eroding today. If you can't affiliate with others while they are yet small and be a part of their growth process with genuineness in your heart, you have no business being a company while they become great! Our 'Why' is as important as our 'What' all the time. There was never a time that he paid more attention to the healthy above the sick There was never a time he paid more attention to the greatest giver than the mite giver. There' was never a point in time that he paid more attention to one status above the other. Jesus hates hypocrisy. This is the sole reason Jesus would tell some people "Get away from me, I know you not" on the last day. Let your preaching match your living. The same energy used in preaching it should be Channeled to doing and living it... I do hope these dots connect though...
