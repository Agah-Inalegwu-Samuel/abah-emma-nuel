THE BODY OF CHRIST


Building has many parts, as well as many responsibilities. There are Land Surveyor, Architect, Building Engineer, Bricklayer, Water fetcher, Blocks molders, Carpenter, Painter, Electrician etc who all have their roles to perform to make the building a reality. We all have our roles, and we are all important. All denominations are like different workers on the same building. Different emphasis, we all tend to overrate our own beat and downplay the Assignment of others.
God's kingdom is liking to a building. God is the owner, Holy Spirit is the Supervisor, while Jesus is the Sponsor. Your church is not the total church of Christ. She's just a part, we all have strength and weaknesses, let's understand our roles and stop working at cross-purposes. The building is only one, and we are all equally hired! The owner expect you to face what you are hired for, not to be another Supervisor. Face your work. A friend told me his experience. He hated the preaching of Joyce Meyer so much. " This woman should preach the real gospel. The gospel is about Salvation, Heaven and Hell, Sin, Spirituality, Holiness etc. Why is she always encouraging people? This is fake, it's not the real gospel" anytime he sees Joyce Meyer, he's already offended. One day, he had serious life challenges and was at the verge of committing suicide, though a GO of Ministry. In his depressed state, he stumbled on Joyce Meyer preaching her normal" encouragement gospel" . My friend listened and it's like God himself speaking to him. He was greatly encouraged, motivated and rescued from suicide, by the same message and messenger he had despised for years. That message you don't need is what someone desperately need. Do your own, and let others do theirs. Everyone is answerable to God.

PROGRESSION IN RECESSION!! 🏢🚖🚘😇


I got suspicious when I learned that prophet Elijah was asked to move out of Brook Kidron because it dried up, for it had not rained for a long time. My studious and inquisitive mind kept saying, "Please tell me another reason".
They were to prepare their last meal, eat it and die. So God made sure that He ran the brook out so Elijah could move to Zarephath to help sustain the lives of the widow and her fatherless son. From God's language, it looked like Elijah was going to be helped by the widow but in reality it was the widow that was helped to survive the recession by the presence of prophet Elijah. So my conclusion is, God brought Elijah out from the brook to save lives outside there. Jesus referring to it said, "There were many widows in Israel in the days of Elijah but unto none of them was Elijah sent but to the widow of Zarephath." Therefore make no boast about how blessed you are in your brook when no one is benefitting in your blessing. In this time of recession in our nation, please look out through your window of comfort and help a needy neighbour to survive. Don't wait for your Brook to dry up before you make that move. May God help us to do the right things in Jesus' name. Amen. Couldn't God who did that keep the brook springing up for Elijah all through the drought? I got meditating and came to place my finger on something outstanding. The man of God was surviving the recession while a widow and her son were down to their last meal. The raven brought Elijah bread and flesh every morning and evening without fail.

RE-AWAKENING!


While laying emphasis on corporate Christian life, the people’s personal walk with the Lord has increasingly gone on the decline. Church attendance has become the hallmark for a healthy Christian life. You really do wonder why myriads of people gather weekly in buildings and yet God is missing in our streets, neighborhoods and territories. The light of most believers seems to only shine inside church walls. A different civilization is dominating our schools, political space and every institution. It will amaze you to know the number of believers that don’t have a personal prayer and study life. When you engage them, you discover how shallow and empty many are. Our corporate life should be an aggregation of who we are individually. Your personal walk with the Lord is more important than your corporate life. It was never Jesus’s desire for the church to become an institutionalized group. We were suppose to be an organic element on earth. Many believers have no life outside church, no walk with the Lord outside corporate duties. We have entered a season where it shall be all man for himself, it is the God that you know that will sustain you this season, corporate life will be rarely impossible. Only those who know their God shall be strong! Selah! Maranatha
