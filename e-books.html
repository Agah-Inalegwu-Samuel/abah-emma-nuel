...REVEALING JESUS, PROMOTING KINGDOM

#ABSOLUTE_FOCUS_IN_THESE_TIMES.


Never forget what it means to live, - To live is Christ. All that you are is Christ. To forget this, is to chase after the wind and become vain. Be deliberate about living for christ.
Never forget how you began, - There's a growing tendency among Christians to suddenly break their spiritual disciplines and routines bcus they heard another man (who had some 'results') say
"such and such is not necessary". We may all be Christians but everyone of us is unique in certain We may all be Christians but everyone of us is unique in certain dealings with God. In spiritual things, it's not everything that's good for the goose that would be good for the gander. Discern yourself and stay with God. He knows what you need to keep doing so you can become what he has intended for you before you were born. You're not in competition with your brother. You'll not be measured by his performance. RUN your RACE and finish YOUR COURSE. Never forget who you met.- Your encounters with God, no matter "how 'little" or "insignificant" they may seem, are proofs that he exists and that you're valuable to him. He showed you those things or gave you those visions as a "bait" to keep you on the path of spiritual progress. Never compromise the authority of scriptures.- Remember anything that does not line up with the spirit inspired and spirit breath word is dangerous. Extra biblical and extra scriptural experiences will most likely lead you outside the jurisdiction of God. Love. Love. Love.- keep loving Jesus. Regardless of what happens around you. If nothing can affect your love for him, you'd never break ranks with Him. Keep the flames of his love raging in your heart. Let nothing competes with your love for him. Not even your desire to live. Hmm. Finally; Find your tribe and stay with your company.- You must find and stay with your company. You're not designed to grow in every environment. Find yours and be committed Therein. Think on these things. What use is it that you began but never finished?

- By Abah Emma Nuel
